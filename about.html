ABOUT US

Meet The Team

Prof. T. Debey Sayndee

Prof. T. Debey Sayndee is the founding director of the Kofi Annan Institute for Conflict Transformation, University of Liberia, and a professor in peace and conflict studies. A leading practitioner in community development, he has worked on the nexus between peace, conflict, security, development, and gender in Liberia and Sierra Leone for over 20 years. He has led the establishment of think tanks in security sector reform, gender, and development, and conducts research and training in empowerment and women's leadership at the community level. He currently supports an M.A. Degree program in Peace Studies and Conflict Transformation at the Kofi Annan Institute for Conflict Transformation.

Dr. Raphaela T. Kormoll

Dr. Raphaela Kormoll is an Associate Professor and Acting Deputy Director for Research at the Kofi Annan Institute for Conflict Transformation, University of Liberia. She was seconded to the institute through the German Civil Peace Service in January 2022 to support the institute's organizational development and research activities. She has been teaching research methodology at undergraduate and master's levels at the University of Durham (United Kingdom) and the University of Liberia (Liberia) and is leading the development and implementation of research projects at the Kofi Annan Institute for Conflict Transformation. Her research creatively combines sociological, anthropological, geographical, historical, and political science methods. Her work focuses on everyday economic and security practices, community development, and peace and conflict studies. She has conducted research in India, Pakistan, and Liberia.
